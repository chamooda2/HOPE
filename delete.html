Account Deletion - HOPE App

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the side drawer menu in the HOPE app.
  • Select Account Deletion.
  • You’ll be redirected to the Account Deletion screen.
  • Read the warning and confirm.
  • Tap the red Delete Account button.
  • Your account will be permanently deleted and you will be signed out.
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. All your data will be removed and cannot be restored.
