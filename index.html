HOPE - Account Deletion Process

How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your HOPE account, you can do so directly from within the app. Follow these steps:

  • Open the side drawer menu inside the HOPE app.
  • Select Account Deletion from the menu options.
  • You will be taken to the Account Deletion screen.
  • On this screen, you’ll see a warning message and the conditions of deletion.
  • Tap the Delete Account button to proceed.
  • Once confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted and you will be signed out of the app.
⚠️ Please note: Account deletion is permanent. All your data associated with HOPE will be removed and cannot be restored.

If you face any issues while deleting your account, please contact our support team at:
